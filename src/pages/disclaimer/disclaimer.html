PLEASE TAKE NOTE

This Application is in no way a replacement for your dietician, or doctor. It does not prescribe any medicines. The application provides dietary guidelines for Pregnant women, infants, Children and Adults with Cystic Fibrosis. The Diet Monitoring is also provided for a single user.

Content of diets and meal ideas presented in this app has been derived from the Cystic Fibrosis Trust, Londong, England. Most of these information are also present at online publications by them.
