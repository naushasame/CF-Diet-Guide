This application represents a Dietary guideline for Cystic Fibrosis Patients. You will be able to get dietary guideline for patients in these categories:

  • Adults
  • Kids
  • Pregnant Women
  • Infants

In addition to this, you can

  • Add your meals, to monitor them approprately
  • Add enzyme supplement
  • Add Height and Weight, to keep track of BMI
  • Add blood sugar readings
  • Add Vitamins intake
  • View Statistics on these data

Much of the content of this app has been based on the publications of Cystic Fibrosis Trust, a charity organization. The Cystic Fibrosis Trust provides information about cystic fibrosis through their factsheets, leaflets and other publications. Most of their publications are available through their website link for online publications.
